About

Blue Hotel and Resort started their operation way back in May of 2011, and in this April of this year, the resort was bought by Jobelle Paula Cho. It is Located in Barangay Lalakay Los Banos, Laguna. The resort is known for having a Korean restaurant with natural hot springs, jacuzzis, swimming pools and a live band performance every Saturday. The resort accommodates guest 24/7, easy to locate, peaceful and has a relaxing vibe that is why Blue Hotel and Resort is a perfect place to spend time with your family and friends and a suitable place for company team building.
